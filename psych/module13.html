What is the Biology of Emotional Disorder?

  Stress can be advantageous as it encourages us to reach our objectives, making us healthier, smarter and happier people. However, too much of a good thing can be a bad thing too much stress can lead to allostatic load.

Allostatic Load:

  Allostatic load is the cumulative physical and psychological wear and tear our bodies experience as a result of chronic stress. Managing allostatic load is essential to maintaining physical and mental health. To do so, it is important to identify and address the sources of stress in your life, practice self-care activities to reduce stress, and cultivate healthy coping strategies to manage stress more effectively. Additionally, getting regular exercise, eating a balanced diet, and getting adequate sleep can help reduce the strain of allostatic load on the body. By taking steps to manage allostatic load, you can protect your physical and mental wellbeing.

Stress:

  The adrenal gland is a rapid responder to stress, releasing glucocorticoids (GCs) and catecholamines into the bloodstream, which affects metabolism by slowing it down, causes an increase in blood pressure, and prevent the immune system from working properly. Stress can also make you feel hungry even when you may not be. The amygdala also plays a role in stress, sending out a distress signal that activates the sympathetic nervous system through the autonomic nerves to the adrenal gland. To protect against the effects of chronic stress, activities like meditation or even just breathing exercises can be beneficial.

Role of the Amygdala:

  The amygdala's part in memory formation is that it is activated by emotional arousal, which leads to the alteration of memory storage in other brain regions. However, when stressed, people find it more difficult to form short-term memories and store them as long-term ones. Additionally, chronic stress can cause overstimulation of the brain, leading to forgetfulness. The hippocampus is also affected by extreme or chronic stress, as its neurochemicals, neural activity, and neurogenesis can be altered, influencing learning and memory and resulting in mental issues. Using the same techniques as mentioned before you can certainly relieve much of this stress before it has time to build and cause damage.
